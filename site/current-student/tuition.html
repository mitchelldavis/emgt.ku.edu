Tuition and Fees

Tuition and fees at the University of Kansas can be found at:

  • Edwards Campus Web Site: http://edwardscampus.ku.edu/ (follow links for "Cost & Aid")
  • University of Kansas Tuition: http://www.tuition.ku.edu
  • Registrar's Web site: http://www.registrar.ku.edu/fees

Metro KC Tuition Rate

The KU Edwards Campus Metro KC tuition rate offers in-state tuition rates to Missouri residents who qualify. No credit hour limit applies.

KU Edwards Campus Tuition Reduction Eligibility Requirements

  • KU Edwards Campus in-state tuition rates for Missouri residents only applies to upper-level (junior/senior) undergraduate and graduate level courses offered on the KU Edwards Campus in Overland Park, Kansas.
  • Missouri students who are admitted degree seeking to an Edwards Campus degree program or non-degree seeking, and who enroll in KU Edwards Campus course(s).
  • Missouri residents of Bates, Buchanan, Cass, Clay, Clinton, Henry, Jackson, Johnson, Lafayette, Platte or Ray counties for the past 12 consecutive months are eligible to apply. Documentation will be required with application.
